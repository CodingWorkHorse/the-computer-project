How We Keep Donations Safe

We understand that these are anything but normal times. As such, we are taking precautions to ensure your safety. Here's what we are doing to keep you safe during the donation process:

  • Appropriate PPE use during all donations
  • Donations are throughly sanitized
  • Offering contactless pick-up
  • Allowing flexible donation strategies
  • Wiping all donated devices to protect your data

      • If there is anything else that we can do to make you feel more comfortable during the donation process, please to hesitate to contact us.
